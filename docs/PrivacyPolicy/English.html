Privacy Policy

Tomoboo (hereinafter referred to as "our company") has established a privacy policy regarding the handling of personal information acquired by us, and this privacy policy, the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (hereinafter referred to as the "Personal Information Protection Act") and related We promise to comply with laws and regulations and handle personal information appropriately.

[Personal Information]

During our business activities, we may acquire information such as terminal identifiers, mobile terminal identifiers, IP addresses, cookies, etc., in addition to personal information defined in Article 2, Paragraph 1 of the Personal Information Protection Law.

[Acquisition of personal information]

When acquiring personal information, we will clarify the purpose of use and will not acquire personal information by deception or other wrongful means.

[Purpose of Use]

Personal information will be used to the extent necessary to achieve the following purposes, and will not be used for any other purpose.

  • To use authentication services for identity verification or identity verification
  • For billing charges
  • To respond to opinions, requests, inquiries, etc.
  • To conduct surveys, campaigns, events, etc. for our services
  • To serve advertisements by the Company or third parties that have commissioned advertisements to the Company
  • For marketing research and analysis, such as usage status of our services and implementation measures
  • For improving the quality of our services, and for research and analysis to improve our services
  • For research and analysis for planning new services and functions of the Company
  • To investigate and respond to system failures, defects, and accidents in our services and networks
  • Other purposes for the smooth operation of our services

[Provision to third parties]

We will not provide personal information to third parties unless we have the prior consent of the individual (including cases where there are special provisions in our terms of service) or in the following cases.

  • When disclosing to financial institutions, credit card companies, or other payment service providers for payment of usage charges
  • When we outsource all or part of our business to a third party
  • When disclosing to a third party who has signed a confidentiality agreement
  • When sharing for advertising purposes
  • When disclosure is permitted by the Personal Information Protection Act or other laws
  • When a court, the police, an administrative agency such as a tax office, or a public agency such as a bar association requests disclosure based on laws and regulations

[Disclosure, correction, deletion, etc.]

If you wish to disclose, correct, add, or delete personal information based on the Personal Information Protection Law or other laws, please contact us at the email address listed in [Contact Us].

[Safety control measures]

In addition to striving to keep personal information accurate and up-to-date, we have established internal regulations to ensure the safe management of personal information. We will strive to take rational and strict security control measures in terms of both aspects and organization.

[Retention period]

The Company will determine the retention period of personal information within the scope necessary to achieve the purpose of use, and after the retention period has elapsed or the purpose of use has been achieved, personal information will be deleted without delay. However, this does not apply in the following cases.

  • When we must save based on the law
  • When consent is obtained from the individual
  • When we determine that there is a reasonable reason to store personal information

[Response in case of leakage]

In the event that personal information is leaked, we will promptly investigate the facts and take appropriate measures such as notifying the person concerned.

[Continuous Improvement]

We will strive to continuously improve the handling of personal information through internal audits and employee training.

[Revision of Privacy Policy]

We may revise our privacy policy without notice. If we revise our privacy policy, we will notify you on our website, so please check it regularly.
